Pitbull

Pitbull's World Cup entry in the atlas is We Are One (Ole Ola), the official song associated with Brazil 2014 and performed with Jennifer Lopez and Claudia Leitte. The collaboration anchors his profile in a specific organiser-labelled release rather than in general football-themed music.

Profile scope

This page is a focused navigation record. It documents the 2014 credit, the collaborating performers, and the status language used by FIFA. Wikidata is linked to identify the artist, not as a substitute for the organiser's music evidence. Listening and release details remain on the song page, where they can be checked without copying lyrics, videos, or platform descriptions.

World Cup music context

FIFA's retrospective anthem playlist includes We Are One, and a later FIFA release again describes it as the official song of the 2014 tournament. Those sources help separate the track from unofficial fan favourites and from Pitbull's later FIFA Club World Cup work. The atlas currently indexes only the men's World Cup entry, while keeping the later reference as supporting context rather than creating an unrelated page.
