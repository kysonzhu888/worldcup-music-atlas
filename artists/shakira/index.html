Shakira

Shakira is one of the clearest multi-tournament links in this atlas. Her entries connect the 2010 South Africa cycle, where Waka Waka became a defining official-song memory, with the 2026 cycle through Dai Dai, a new official song credited jointly with Burna Boy.

Profile scope

This page stays focused on the documented tournament connection rather than reproducing a general celebrity biography. FIFA's 2026 release confirms the status and artist credit for Dai Dai, while FIFA's retrospective playlist places Waka Waka in the organisation's World Cup anthem history. The linked Wikidata record is used only to disambiguate the artist identity. Song-level pages contain the detailed release notes and listening links.

World Cup music context

The sixteen-year gap between the two indexed releases makes this profile useful for comparing how World Cup music changed. Waka Waka sits in the era of one globally dominant tournament song and a highly recognisable video. Dai Dai arrives in a broader official-album program, where one headline song is surrounded by many cross-market collaborations. The two entries should therefore be compared as products of different release strategies, not treated as interchangeable versions of the same role.
