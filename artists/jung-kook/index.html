Jung Kook

Jung Kook appears through Dreamers, a Qatar 2022 official-soundtrack release connected directly to the tournament's opening. The atlas pairs the recording with Fahad Al Kubaisi's ceremony credit and keeps the page centred on that documented World Cup moment.

Profile scope

This profile does not attempt to summarise BTS or Jung Kook's wider career. It uses FIFA's release as the primary record for the song, date, collaborators, and ceremony relationship. Wikidata is an identity reference. The song page carries listening links and fuller tournament context, while this page provides a stable artist route that can receive a dated update if another verified World Cup credit appears.

World Cup music context

FIFA released Dreamers at the start of the tournament and announced that Jung Kook would perform it with Qatari singer Fahad Al Kubaisi during the opening ceremony. That makes the entry both a soundtrack release and a ceremony moment. The profile retains the more specific opening-ceremony label used in the atlas while linking to FIFA's broader soundtrack explanation for readers comparing it with the other 2022 singles.
