Davido

Davido connects two consecutive World Cup cycles in the atlas. He is credited on Hayya Hayya (Better Together), the first single from Qatar 2022's multi-song official soundtrack, and on No Place Like Home, an official-album track in the 2026 collection.

Profile scope

The profile is built from organiser announcements and the entries already maintained in the atlas. FIFA's 2022 release explicitly names Trinidad Cardona, Davido, and Aisha, while the 2026 album announcement provides the later program context. Wikidata is included as an identity reference. General chart history, unrelated releases, and social-media claims are outside this page's scope unless they directly clarify a documented World Cup music connection.

World Cup music context

These two credits show why precise release labels matter. Hayya Hayya introduced FIFA's 2022 soundtrack program and was presented around the final draw. No Place Like Home belongs to the larger 2026 official album rather than being labelled the tournament's main official song. Keeping those categories separate helps readers understand the artist's recurring World Cup connection without exaggerating the status of either track.
