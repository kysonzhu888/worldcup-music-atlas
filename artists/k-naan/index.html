K'naan

K'naan's atlas profile is built around Wavin' Flag, a 2010 campaign song that became inseparable from many fans' World Cup memories. Its inclusion also illustrates an important editorial distinction: cultural recognition does not automatically make a track FIFA's main official tournament song.

Profile scope

The page records the artist identity, the campaign relationship, and the 2010 cycle. It does not use social popularity as proof of official status. The Coca-Cola announcement and FIFA retrospective provide complementary evidence, while Wikidata helps disambiguate the artist. Any future claims about charts, regional versions, or performances should be added only with a dated source and a new content-update timestamp.

World Cup music context

Coca-Cola's campaign release is the primary evidence for the celebration-mix role, while FIFA's retrospective playlist shows how firmly the track entered World Cup music memory. The atlas therefore labels Wavin' Flag as a campaign song and avoids collapsing it into Waka Waka's official-song category. That difference is useful for readers comparing what organisers released with what sponsors and supporters popularised.
