Ricky Martin

Ricky Martin's atlas entry centres on The Cup of Life from the 1998 tournament cycle. It is one of the site's historical reference points for the era when a single multilingual pop release could become the most recognisable musical shorthand for an entire World Cup.

Profile scope

This profile is intentionally tournament-specific. FIFA's retrospective selection supports the music connection, and Wikidata identifies the performer. The atlas writes its own context and sends readers to licensed listening destinations; it does not reproduce lyrics or a full biography. Historical details beyond the song's World Cup role remain candidates until they are supported by primary archives or reputable contemporaneous reporting.

World Cup music context

The page treats The Cup of Life as a classic official song and links it to the France 1998 cycle. FIFA's later anthem playlist includes the track under its Spanish title, La Copa de la Vida, with the English title alongside it. That title variation is also a useful maintenance lesson: weekly discovery tools may surface both versions, so a human reviewer must merge aliases rather than publishing a duplicate page.
