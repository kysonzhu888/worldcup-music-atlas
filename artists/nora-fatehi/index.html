Nora Fatehi

Nora Fatehi appears in two atlas entries across consecutive tournament cycles: Light The Sky from the Qatar 2022 official soundtrack and Siir Siir from the 2026 official album. Together they form a compact example of FIFA's shift toward larger, multi-artist music programs.

Profile scope

The profile limits itself to the two connections supported by FIFA material and the current atlas records. The organiser releases establish the program labels; Wikidata provides an identity reference. It does not copy social biographies or treat hashtags as evidence. A future weekly review can update the profile when FIFA changes track metadata or when a new authoritative source clarifies the live-performance history.

World Cup music context

Light The Sky was released with an all-female lineup and sits within the multi-song 2022 soundtrack. Siir Siir is catalogued as a 2026 official-album track with Vegedream and Sanjoy. Neither entry should be casually promoted as the single definitive tournament song. Their value here is the continuity of the artist credit across two different official collections and the contrast between the soundtrack and album labels.
