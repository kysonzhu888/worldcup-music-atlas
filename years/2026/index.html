Final week: three music roles to track

The Closing Ceremony starts 90 minutes before kick-off, while the Final Halftime Show happens during the match interval. Both have confirmed performers, but neither announcement supplies a complete song order. These notes keep the event and music-role labels separate.

Official song Dai Dai

FIFA confirms Shakira and Burna Boy in the Final Halftime Show lineup on 19 July. The complete song-by-song set list has not been published, so Dai Dai remains the official tournament song without a confirmed halftime performance.

Official anthem DNA

DNA's verified role remains the tournament-wide official anthem. FIFA's checked release ties its first live performance to the Mexico City opening ceremony on 11 June; this atlas does not claim a separate final performance without a FIFA source.

Official album track Game Time

Game Time's verified role remains an official album track with an opening-ceremony connection. FIFA announced its live debut for Los Angeles on 12 June; this atlas does not promote it to a final song without a FIFA source.
