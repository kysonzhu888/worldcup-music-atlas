Global World Cup music

The Global collection in this atlas spans 2010 to 2026, based on the entries currently documented here.

What this collection contains

  • 18 documented entries across two tournament cycles.
  • 18 entries are identified as official or classic tournament music in the source data.
  • The indexed language labels are Arabic, English, Multilingual, Portuguese, and Spanish.
  • 40 credited artists or groups appear across these entries.
  • The collection's country labels are Global.

The counts and labels come from the entries currently published in this atlas. “Official”, “soundtrack”, “campaign”, and “fan” are kept separate because they describe different relationships to a tournament; a popular song is not automatically an organiser-designated release.

Collection content updated: 2026-07-03. New discoveries enter a review queue before they can change these totals.
