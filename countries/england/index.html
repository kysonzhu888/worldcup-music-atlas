England World Cup music

The England collection in this atlas spans 1996, based on the entries currently documented here.

What this collection contains

  • 1 documented entry across one tournament cycle.
  • 1 entry is identified as official or classic tournament music in the source data.
  • The indexed language labels are English.
  • 3 credited artists or groups appear across these entries.
  • The collection's country labels are England.

The counts and labels come from the entries currently published in this atlas. “Official”, “soundtrack”, “campaign”, and “fan” are kept separate because they describe different relationships to a tournament; a popular song is not automatically an organiser-designated release.

Collection content updated: 2026-07-03. New discoveries enter a review queue before they can change these totals.

Three Lions

Baddiel, Skinner, The Lightning Seeds

Three Lions is not a FIFA World Cup official song, but its 'coming home' refrain has become one of the most recognizable sounds around England at major tournaments.
